I am a 2nd level national athlete in badminton(state champion).

I started training since eight and later joined varsity. At age 16, I competed in a state-wide competition, won the 1st place, and got the 2nd level national athlete title.

The sweetest memory is that there was always a crowd around the court where I trained. This is probably because of the contrast of my fierce strokes with my tiny figure haha.

A fun fact is that my strategy is perseverance. I don't get tired and recover in a surprsingly fast speed.
