I studied drawing and piano for years when I was little and continue to appreciate the arts and beauties in life since then.

Music

My favorite Chinese composor is Hai Lin. 林海作曲缠绵悱恻却又荡气回肠， 绵里藏针，于细微处见深情。有一种即使命运悲剧也满怀热爱的凄壮，对我而言体现了中华民族精神世界的意象。 This Guzheng piece called Pipa Language has remained my favorite since when I first heard it at age 10.

My favorite foreign composer is Chopin. I was speechless when I first heard Nocturne op.9 No.2 Such a divine work! 真切体会到了“曾经沧海难为水， 除却巫山不是云”和“如听仙乐耳暂明”，以往的音乐一瞬间变得“呕哑嘲哳难为听”。

Painting

My favorite Impressionism painter is Renoir. I would fly to Philly just to enjoy his paintings. His painting gives me a sense of great joy out of no where. Very mysterious and marvelous.

My favorite Neoclassicism painter is Ingres. His paintings often give me the impression that they are too perfect to exist. I was stunned when I saw his The Princesse de Broglie for the first time at the Met. His Grande Odalisque has long time been my favorite painting.

Apart from appreciating arts, I also create my own by painting and photography.

Cultural Aesthetic

Wuxia is one key specialty in Chinese culture. There is some spiritual space special for Chinese. It's hard to explain in English and to explain to someone who is not grown in this culture. Will come back when I'm capable of doing a better job.

An disclaimer is that aesthetic is very subjective. It only reflects my personal point of view/impression. I have not conducted thorough research in Chinese culture so if you are serious about learning more about Chinesse culture, don't rely solely on my words.
